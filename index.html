FAQ

Why power over crit?

Koreans tell you that crit is better than power because u get guaranteed damage. What they are saying is partially true, but not for gunners. The problem in Ktera is that dps meter are not allowed, so they cannot accurately determine every class’s dps unlike NA Tera where the dps meter is allowed. According to multiple tests, with 180 total crit rate, time bomb has 100% crit rate (if done correctly, read question 2), arcane barrage has 100% crit rate and burst fire has 80% crit rate. Arcane barrage is 30% of your damage, burst fire is also 30% of your damage and time bomb is 10% of your damage. So we can assume that arcane barrage, burst fire and time bomb counts as 70% of your damage and the other 30% is the rest of your skills. Also, don’t get me started on scattershot, that skill is complete trash, it should only be used as a willpower generation/mobility/filler skill, so there’s no point in building full crit in order to get 80% crit rate on a FILLER skill. In short, try to aim for 180 total crit rate and then from there on build as much power as you want.

How do I get 100% crit rate on Time Bomb and HB summon?

One important thing to know is that burst fire acts as a crit rate amplifier the more you use it. Usually, when you burst fire with full willpower, you get 100% crit rate on your burst fire at around half of your willpower. You can take advantage of this by applying this logic to other skills that have a delay such as time bomb or hb summon. As you may know, when you cast time bomb, there is an initial delay before the bomb explodes. While the bomb is exploding, you can build up your crit rate by burst firing right after you cast it, so after around 3 seconds, u are at about 100% crit rate which allows you to crit on every single one of your time bombs. You can also do this with hb summon. In short, time bomb>hb summon>burst fire

Are CDR etchings good?

CDR etchings isn’t bad, but is not optimal for PvE. If you were pvping, cdr would be better than double pumped or double keen. The utility of cdr etchings is to do your rotations faster, because the cdr etchings has cooldown reduction stats and attack speed stats (which synergies with each other). If you have too much cooldown but not enough attack speed, you will find that a lot of your skills are up before you even finish your rotation. The problem with gunner is that they don’t benefit from attack speed, so cdr etchings basicly only gives you cooldown reduction but no attack speed. The best gunner rotation for the maximum dps does not require cooldown reduction. Also, the main reason people build cdr on gunner is to use ab more often. The problem with that is it doesn’t actually boost your damage, because gunners don’t rely 100% on arcane barrage, so a lot of times, I have ab up but I don’t cast it because in using burst fire. If you were to go full cdr, that means u also need to roll 15% less cooldown on replenishment on your chest roll. Pumped or keen etchings is the way to go, since it gives you more damage in you burstfire/arcane barrage which is 60% of your dps (30% for ab and 30% for burst fire).
